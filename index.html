About Me

Welcome to my Bioinformatics Portfolio!

I am a bioinformatics scientist with a robust foundation in data analysis, computational biology, and statistical modeling. My expertise spans across programming languages such as R and Python, enabling me to develop and optimize bioinformatics pipelines for high-throughput sequencing data, including RNA-seq, single-cell transcriptomics, and circular RNA analysis.
